Our mission

Our coding school is dedicated to helping women overcome the significant challenges they face in the tech industry. By providing targeted support and resources, we aim to close the gender gap and empower women to succeed in STEM careers.

  • Women Represent 35% of the STEM Workforce in the U.S.:
    Despite progress, women remain underrepresented in tech. Our school aims to increase this percentage by equipping women with the skills needed to thrive in the industry.
  • Gender gap in major tech companies:
    At top tech companies like Google, Apple, and Microsoft, women make up only 33-45% of the workforce and even fewer in leadership roles. We aim to support women in breaking through these barriers and achieving leadership positions.
  • Hiring bias:
    65% of tech recruiters acknowledge bias in hiring, and many women face unclear career paths. Our platform provides mentorship and interview preparation to help women navigate these challenges and advance in their careers.
  • Women more affected by layoffs:
    Women in tech are 1.6 times more likely to be laid off, and many leave due to poor work-life balance. Our schoolâ€™s flexible, self-paced learning and career support will help women build more stable and fulfilling careers.
  • The pay gap:
    Women in tech earn $15,000 less than men on average. By providing access to quality education and professional development, we aim to equip women to bridge this pay gap and earn equal wages for equal work.

Through our tailored courses, mentorship, and career support, we are committed to empowering women to overcome these statistics and succeed in the tech world.
